Structural Factors Marketing and Sales Workplace Enviroment

Provide a brief description of the environment in which workers undertake their duties.

Microsoft aims to build a diverse and inclusive environment to benefit all employees. The firm offers benefits and ways for employees to stay healthy, counitine there education and give back to the community.

How is the quality of the goods and/or services controlled in the business?

The quality of goods and services is determined by project managers when developing a service and by the factory when a product is produced.

List the main tools (hardware, software, gadgets) used by the company.

As Microsoft is an IT company the firm uses computers to complete work, whether that is graphic design or AI development.

Describe the function of the major tools used.

The firm leverages computers to complete work, referring to basic pcbs with processors or workstation desktop computers.

How has the development of new technology affected this firm?

As Microsoft operates in the IT sector new developments directly affect them. For example, as Microsoft produces video game systems (Xbox) new graphics upgrades and other improvements in the technology they use directly affects whether consumers will purchase their product or that of a competitor.

If mass production is used as a production method in the organisation describe the areas where it is used and explain the reasons and benefits for using this method.

Microsoft mass produces computers for their line of ‘surface’ laptops and they also mass produce the Xbox. This benefits the firm as it allows then to meet consumer demand and achieve economies of scale.

What impacts does the resources used by the business have on the environment and society?

Microsoft uses toxic materials, such as, the toxic chemicals incorporated into pcbs and plastics. The firm also consumes power that may not be from a clean energy source.

What are the alternatives to the resources mentioned above.

Alternatives to plastics, such as, metals and wood could be used by the firm. The firm could also source clean power, such as solar, wind or hydro.

Does the firm have any waste products?

The main waste products the firm produces are general office waste and old hardware, such as old computers.

What is done with all the waste products?

General office waste is disposed of by the local council while old hardware is taken to e waste centres.

How many people are employed in the organisation?

Microsoft employees over 131 thousand people.

List the jobs and roles.

Given the firms large scale there are many positions and roles within the firm, some of these roles include: management roles (financial management, project management), design (graphic, cad), video production (directors, lighting, camera operators) and engineering (software, physical).

Describe how employees in the business negotiate wages and conditions with the employer.

Microsoft employees negotiate wages and conditions in the same way negotiations would take place in a standard firm.
