What does Microsoft do?

Microsoft best known for their operating system Windows and Office 365 also specialises in social media, gaming, cloud computing and AI. The social media platform, LinkedIn allows millions of business professionals to connect and share ideas across the globe. Microsoft are currently investing in the streaming service Mixer, cloud computing and AI as part of the to build a world class platform for both PC and Xbox. Microsoft is also branching into cloud computing with their service, Azure. The company is also investing and developing AI to assist and improve the usability of all of their services.

How and when was Microsoft established?

Microsoft was founded in 1975, on the 4th of April, Albuquerque, New Mexico, United States. The organization was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen. Originally developing software for the Altair 8800 the company then went on to produce and licence the MS-DOS operating system to IBM.

Is Microsoft Australia wide or global in nature?

The organisation is a global in nature providing services across the world.The firms headquarters is based in Washington, United States of America.
